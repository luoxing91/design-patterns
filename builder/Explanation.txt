Builder pattern allows separete creation object procedure 
from its reprezetation.

Use:
When you want to construct complicated objects.


Actors roles:

Client
Wants to specyfic product dont know how to build it.
To obtain specyfic product have to choose algorytm (Director)
and specyfication (Builder) which contain a details how to
build certain part of product.

Director
Knowns nothing what he build he knows only algorithm.
Knowns how to build product but dont knowns the detail
He use concrete builder to build product according to
his algorithm.

Builder
Knowns details how to build product part according to
procedure which he is treated by Direcor provide 
diffrent product.
